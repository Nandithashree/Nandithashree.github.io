ABC School

ABC School is located in Yelachenahalli in Bengaluru. It spreads over an area of 1000 acres. There are separate buildings for Pre-Primary,Primary Middle school secondary school and senior secondary wings. The school is run by an efficient management and has well qualified staff members. The school is affliated to CBSE and ISO certified.
